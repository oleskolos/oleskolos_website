About

Oles Kolos is a Ukrainian goalkeeper. He was born on March 2, 2003 in Kyiv, Ukraine.

Profile

The Kolos height is 187 cm. And weight is 87 kg. The working leg is right.

First Steps

Oles Kolos made first steps in his football career in "FC Zirka Kyiv". He was there for 11 years.

U-19 Team

After graduating from Academy, Kolos was going to "FC Skylark Kyiv", where he was playing for 1 year as a U-19 footballer.

Career in Adult Teams

Oles Kolos was playing for some teams from different leagues and regions of Ukraine, such as "SC Unior Makariv", "FC Polissya Stavky" and "FC Arsenal Kyiv".

Top Coaches, He Worked With

The most famous coaches with whom Oles worked were Viacheslav Bogodielov, Serhiy Kovalets, Myhaylo Fedunov, Oleksandr Mytko.

Statistics

Career Achievement

The most valuable achievement in Oles Kolos career was in the period when he played for "FC Polissya Stavky". Just only in one season, he won the "Zytomyr Region Cup" and finished in third place in the Zytomyr region championship.
